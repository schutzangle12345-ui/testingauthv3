Our Mission

Trust shouldn't be a chore 🎈

We believe SaaS teams should spend their time building incredible features, not wrestling with SIG questionnaires and enterprise security gatekeepers.

Why we started testingauthv3

We saw too many brilliant SaaS founders lose mid-market and enterprise deals because they couldn't answer 300-row Excel spreadsheets about their security posture.

Security assessments used to be the domain of massive consulting firms charging $50k a pop. Small teams couldn't afford a full-time compliance hire, so they just... stayed small. We built testingauthv3 to level the playing field.

Our platform automates the heavy lifting of evidence gathering, maps your controls to SIG, CAIQ, and SOC2, and generates a beautiful, shareable security packet that enterprises actually trust.
